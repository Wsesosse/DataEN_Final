How the Model Works

Our ML model uses machine learning algorithms to predict GDP growth based on key economic indicators:

  • Base GDP: Starting point for economic size
  • Inflation Rate: Price level changes in economy
  • Unemployment: Labor market health indicator
  • Interest Rate: Borrowing cost and monetary policy
  • Trade Balance: Imports vs exports ratio
  • Investment & Consumption: Demand side drivers
